WHO WE ARE ?

Greater Noida Institute of Technology (GNIOT) is one of the premier Institutions in the field of Technical and Management Education. It has been formed by Shri Ram Educational Trust, Noida . The Trust was formed in the year 2000 and the Institute was established in the year 2001. The Trust has had a meteoric rise, and on date, has established a chain of educational institutions covering the fields of engineering and management leading towards Graduate and Post Graduate degrees.

It has been approved by AICTE, Ministry of HRD, Government of India and affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow .Also accredited with NAAC A+ in first cycle round which represents Strong Academic Framework & Student-Centric Practices.​

Step 1 - Signup

To complete the registration process for CUCET, you are required to provide some essential details, including your full name, a valid email ID, mobile number, state of residence, gender, and a secure password. Your email ID will function as your unique username for accessing the portal, ensuring a seamless login experience. Additionally, you will have the opportunity to set your own password during the registration process, allowing you to create a secure and personalized account for future access.
